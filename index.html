COMPANY OVERVIEW

Topic Laboratories is a leading medical science laboratory located at Sagamu, Ogun State, Nigeria.
Registered in August, 2001 by the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (CAC), the organization was subsequently issued the license to carry out clinical laboratory investigations as well as supply medical equipment, chemicals, reagent and consumables by the Medical laboratory is the diagnostic arm of Topic laboratories

AREAS OF CORE COMPETENCE

With state of the art equipment and qualified personnel, Topic Diagnostic Laboratories is equipped to handle a wide range of medical laboratory investigations including haematology, clinical chemistry and hormonals.
